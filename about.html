Elevating Postnatal Care

At Embun Teratai, we believe that the first weeks of motherhood should be a time of deep rest, healing, and joy. Our centre blends the comfort of a luxury boutique hotel with the expertise of a dedicated medical and wellness team.

Every detail, from our serene suites to our nourishing cuisine, has been carefully curated to provide mothers with a peaceful, supportive environment as they bond with their newborns.

Professional Compassionate Team

Your recovery is supported by our Paediatrician-led medical team, certified nurses, and experienced caregivers. We provide 24/7 care, ensuring both you and your baby receive the highest standard of attention.

Our Certified Postnatal Masseuses are trained in traditional techniques, offering authentic therapies that have nurtured generations of mothers.
